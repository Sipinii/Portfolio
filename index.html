About Me

I'm a third-year video game programming student at Kajaani University of Applied Sciences.

But truthfully, I've been a lifelong video game scholar, reading video game magazines, watching featurettes and interviews since I was a child.

I've also read various game development related books like:

  • Game Mechanics: Advanced Game Design
  • The Art of Game Design: A Book of Lenses
  • A Theory Of Fun For Game Design
I've travelled around the world and plan to continue my adventures somewhere down the line. Meanwhile, I'm creating prototypes and waiting for the next FromSoftware game to be released.
